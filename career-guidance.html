Career Guidance

Charting Your Path After 12th Grade

Explore Your Options

Choosing the right career path after 12th grade is a pivotal decision. Here are some of the most popular and promising pathways for students.

Engineering (B.E. / B.Tech)

One of the most sought-after fields. Ideal for students with a strong grasp of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

Medicine & Allied Health

For students passionate about biology and healthcare. Requires dedication and clearing entrance exams like NEET.

  • MBBS / BDS
  • B.Pharm / D.Pharm
  • Nursing / Physiotherapy
  • Biotechnology

Arts & Science

Diverse opportunities in specialized fields ranging from pure sciences to humanities.

  • B.Sc. (Physics, Chemistry, Maths)
  • B.Com / B.B.A (Commerce & Business)
  • B.A. (English, History, Economics)
  • B.C.A (Computer Applications)

Government & Competitive Exams

Prepare for government services and defense roles early on.

  • TNPSC (Tamil Nadu Public Service)
  • UPSC (Civil Services)
  • NDA (National Defence Academy)
  • Banking Exams
